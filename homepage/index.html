About

Chickenoodle Music is my personal space for sharing the songs and playlists I enjoy the most. It’s not about producing or creating music — it’s about celebrating the tracks that make my life brighter and sharing them with others who feel the same way.


I love music because it has the power to change moods, spark memories, and create new experiences. Whether it’s relaxing with calm tunes, finding energy in upbeat tracks, or discovering sounds from around the world, music has always been a way for me to connect with emotions and people.


Here, you’ll find curated playlists, highlights of favorite genres, and recommendations meant to inspire your own listening journey. Hopefully, you’ll discover your next favorite song here too!
